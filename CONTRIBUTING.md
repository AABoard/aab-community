# Contributing to AAB Community

This repository helps contributors find the right AAB collaboration path. If you are unsure where your contribution belongs, start here with a contributor intake issue.

## Contribution Types

- Joining a working group.
- Suggesting community dialogue topics.
- Offering research, review, teaching, design, data, or engineering support.
- Asking where a case, policy signal, standards comment, or website issue should go.
- Improving onboarding materials.

## Routing Guide

- Data schemas, templates, exports, validation, or search: `aab-data-and-schema`.
- Standards drafts, comments, terminology, or evidence trace: `aab-standards`.
- Governance, roles, policies, and decisions: `aab-governance`.
- General volunteering, working groups, and contributor routing: this repository.

## Safety Rules

Do not submit private student data, credentials, confidential partner documents, unpublished pilot materials, or private contact lists. Public collaboration should use source-traced, reviewable, and privacy-conscious materials.

## Contributor Norms

- Be specific about what you want to help with.
- Identify your relevant expertise or role.
- Link public sources where possible.
- Accept that registry inclusion and standards language require review.
- Keep public discussion respectful, evidence-oriented, and non-promotional.
