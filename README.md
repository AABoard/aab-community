# AABoard Community

This repository is the public collaboration hub for people who want to contribute to the International AI Assessment Board (AABoard). It routes contributors into the right workstream: case review, standards development, research synthesis, policy monitoring, website improvement, community dialogue, and volunteer support.

## What Belongs Here

- Contributor onboarding materials.
- Working group descriptions and coordination notes.
- Public roadmap items.
- Calls for reviewers, researchers, educators, and technical contributors.
- Discussion prompts and community meeting notes.
- Issue intake for people who are unsure where to contribute.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Live MongoDB records.
- Website source code.
- Standards drafts that need formal clause review.
- Governance policy changes that belong in `aab-governance`.
- Sensitive partner, pilot, student, or donor information.

## Repository Structure

- `onboarding/` - how to join and contribute.
- `working-groups/` - descriptions of active contributor groups.
- `roadmap/` - public collaboration priorities.
- `discussions/` - reusable discussion prompts and meeting summaries.
- `volunteer-roles/` - role descriptions for volunteers and contributors.
- `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/` - contributor intake forms.

## Where To Contribute

- Data structures, schemas, exports, and search: `aab-data-and-schema`.
- Standards drafts and public comments: `aab-standards`.
- Governance policies and decision records: `aab-governance`.
- General onboarding and working-group coordination: this repository.

## Contributor Path

1. Read `onboarding/getting-started.md`.
2. Choose a working group or open a contributor intake issue.
3. A maintainer routes the contribution to the right repository or working group.
4. Work proceeds through GitHub issues and pull requests so decisions remain traceable.

## Contributing

Use `CONTRIBUTING.md` for norms, safety rules, and routing guidance. Do not submit private student data, unpublished partner documents, or credentials.
