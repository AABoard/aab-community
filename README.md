# aab-community
Community discussions and collaboration space for the International AI Assessment Board (AAB), including standards development, case registry coordination, research collaboration, and volunteer engagement.

## Community Sections

### 📢 Announcements
Official announcements from the AAB board and leadership. Stay up to date with the latest news, policy changes, and organizational updates.

### 💬 General Discussion
Open forum for general topics related to the AAB community. Share ideas, introduce yourself, or discuss anything that doesn't fit into a specific category.

### 📐 Standards Development
Collaborate on the development and refinement of AI assessment standards. Discuss proposals, provide feedback on drafts, and participate in the standards-setting process.

### 🗂️ Case Registry
Coordinate and discuss AI assessment cases. Share case studies, review submissions, and contribute to building a comprehensive registry of AI assessments.

### 🔬 Research Collaboration
Connect with fellow researchers and collaborate on AI assessment research projects. Share findings, seek collaborators, and coordinate joint research initiatives.

### 🤝 Volunteer Coordination
Find volunteer opportunities within the AAB community. Coordinate working groups, committee participation, and other ways to contribute to the organization's mission.

### ❓ Q&A
Ask questions and get answers from the AAB community. Whether you're new to AI assessment or a seasoned professional, this is the place to seek guidance and share knowledge.
